Cooperative gestures

With cooperativeGestures: true the viewer no longer traps the page's scroll when embedded:

  • Touch: one finger scrolls the page; two fingers pan and zoom the image.
  • Desktop: the mouse wheel scrolls the page; Ctrl (or ⌘) + wheel zooms.
  • Mouse drag still pans, and the restrictions lift automatically in full-page mode.
